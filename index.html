What is Praise NHS?

Praise NHS helps people with spare PPE send them directly to healthcare workers on the front lines who need them in this pandemic.

Their lives (and our lives) depend on it.

Many hospitals have had to limit the number of PPE healthcare workers can use because there isn't enough. There are PPE shortages across the country. Many are forced to go without due to lack of supply. Some are forced to use DIY goggles. Staff feel inadequately protected and vulnerable to infection. While factories domestically and overseas are rushing to produce more, producing masks takes time that many healthcare workers don't have.

They feel unable to carry out their job properly and protect patients. The NHS is hugely understaffed and NHS trusts rely on both permanent and agency staff to do extra shifts to cope with the shortfalls. We can't expect workers to take on extra shifts on a ward dealing with actual & potential COVID-19 patients without adequate PPE.

We may already have masks here - in our closets, garages, basements, and workshops. Maybe you hoarded some and they're just sitting there. Your masks could save someone's life.

How it works?

If you are a healthcare worker, fill out the "I need PPE" form so we know who you are and where we need to send your equipment.

If you have PPE, fill out the "I have PPE" form, and we'll email you with a healthcare worker match so you can ship your masks directly to them.

Our team verifies the identities and sends a match via email to the donor.* The donor ships it out and sends a tracking number. We share the tracking number with the healthcare worker.

*Praise NHS will make sure to verify the identities of the healthcare workers before making any matches. These PPE are not for purchase or for sale. Those who are matched with a healthcare worker are expected to cover the cost of shipping & handling.
